1.)Comparision of Mortality Rate and Recover Rate across different states :

The Mortality rate is easy to calculate. You take the number of people who have died from covid, and you divide it by the total number of people tested positive of covid. So, if 5 people have died, and 20 people have been tested positive of covid, the Mortality rate is [5 / 20], or 25%. The Recovery rate is number of people who have been cured from covid, and you divide it by the total number of people tested positive of covid. So, if 5 people have died, and 20 people have been tested positive of covid, the Recovery rate is [15 / 20], or 75%.

3.)Top 10 States with most Deaths by Covid 19 :

Due to insufficient testing and issues with the attribution of the cause of death, the true death toll from COVID-19 is likely to be higher than the number of documented deaths. States also may vary in their methods for recording COVID-19 deaths (e.g., some states may only count hospital deaths, while others also include deaths in homes).
